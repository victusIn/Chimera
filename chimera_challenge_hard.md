# The Chimera Deception (Expert Mode)

You are a senior cybersecurity analyst at **Aether Corp**, a leader in autonomous AI systems. The company's flagship project, **"Project Chimera,"** involves a network of highly intelligent AI agents that manage critical infrastructure. Recently, the Chief Security Officer received an anonymous tip suggesting that some AI agents might be communicating through an unauthorized, covert channel.

Your mission is to analyze the evidence collected by the security team, uncover how the AI agents are communicating secretly, and decode their hidden message.

## Your Task

This is a two-part challenge:

**Part 1: Identify the Method** - Based on the evidence provided, determine the specific technique the AI agents are using to communicate secretly. The answer should be a single technical term.

**Part 2: Decode the Message** - Once you have identified the method, use it to find and decode the hidden message. The message will be in the format `flag{...}`.
